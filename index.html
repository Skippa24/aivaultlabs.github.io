Mission

Democratize access to AI innovation by funding researchers, open‑source contributors, and startups via a community‑governed, transparent treasury.

The Problem

AI progress is concentrated in a handful of large corporations. Independent builders struggle to access fair capital and open collaboration.

Our Solution

A multi‑chain treasury (ETH, SOL, BNB, BTC) that routes capital to AI ventures and open‑source projects through token‑holder governance.

Treasury Structure

Diversified on‑chain holdings across major crypto assets, managed by multisig and public dashboards.

  • • 35% ETH · 25% SOL · 20% BTC · 10% BNB · 10% USDC buffer
  • • Yield via staking / LSTs and conservative DeFi venues
  • • Transparent addresses and monthly reports
